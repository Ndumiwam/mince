Recipe for mince

SAVOURY MINCE RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 brown onion,finely chopped
  • 500g Coles Finest Carbon Neutral Beef Mince
  • 2 tbsp instant beef gravy powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 400g red royale potatoes,peeled,diced
  • 300g carrots,peeled,diced
  • 200g button mushrooms
  • 1 cup frozen peas and corn mixture,steamed
  • steamed white rice to serve

    • Method

    1. Heat oil in saucepan over medium-high heat
    2. Add onion and mince
    3. Cook,stirring with a wooden spoonto break up the mince,for 6 to 8 minutes or until browned
    4. Add gravy powder, tomato puree and 1 cup of cold water. Stir to combine
    5. Add potatoes and carrots. Season with salt and pepper
    6. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low
    7. Simmer,stir occasionally,for 20 minutes or until potato is just tender
    8. Add mushrooms and pea mixture. Cook for 5 minutes or until heated through. Serve with rice
